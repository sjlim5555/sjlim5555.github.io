About Me:

My name is Seong-Jin Lim. I am an undergraduate student at Arizona State University. This is my 4th and final year of my undergraduate studies and I am a double major pursuing a bachelor's in Supply Chain Management and Computer Information Systems. I love learning, challenges, and spending time with my loved ones. Besides schooling and my career, I LOVE all types of music. I used to play the saxophone for 5 years, but in my spare time now I try and practice my guitar as much as possible.

I have extensive experience within the aerospace industry and manufacturing settings. Having had the opportunity to work in varying factory floors, I have a strong grasp on what it takes to have successful production runs for differing contracts/projects. Throughout my years of schooling and internship experience, I have developed a deep appreciation of the complexities and nuances of supply chain management. Whether it is planning, operations, procurement, or logistics, I am ready to tackle any challenge. Thank you for visiting my page, and feel free to reach out to me for any of your needs.
